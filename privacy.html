Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026  ·  Last updated: July 22, 2026

Life 168 keeps your answers on your own device. It does not create an account, it does not use analytics or advertising, and it does not send your answers to us or to anyone else.

The short version: everything you type or choose in this survey is saved only in your own browser, on your own device. We never see it, and it is never uploaded.

Information the app handles

As you use the survey, the app saves what you enter so you can come back to it:

  • Your written answers in Part 1 (what a great life means to you, your purpose, your 3 to 5 year picture).
  • Your importance and satisfaction ratings and the weekly hours you assign to each of the 16 life areas.
  • Your action plan text in Part 5 and Part 6.
  • The start date you confirm, and whether you opted in to reminders.

Where it is stored and who can see it

All of the above is stored in your browser's local storage, on the device you are using. It stays on that device. It is not transmitted to a server, and the developer has no way to read it. It remains until you remove it, which you can do at any time with the Start over button, by clearing this site's data in your browser, or by uninstalling the app.

Reminders and notifications

Reminders are optional. If you choose to turn them on:

  • Any notification permission is requested and controlled by your device.
  • The 30, 60, and 90 day dates and the calendar file are created on your device.
  • No reminder information is sent to us.

Cookies and tracking

The app uses no cookies, no analytics, no advertising identifiers, and no third party trackers.

Links to other services

The app includes links to a video on YouTube and, as a backup, an audio file on Google Drive. If you choose to open one of those links, you leave the app and the privacy policy of that service (Google) applies to what happens there. The app does not share your survey answers with those services.

Hosting and server logs

Like most websites, the service that hosts this app may automatically record standard technical information, such as your IP address, browser type, and the time of a request, in ordinary server logs used for security and reliability. The app itself does not read this information, does not combine it with your answers, and does not use it to identify you.

Children's privacy

The survey is written for a general audience of every age. Because the app collects nothing on any server, it does not knowingly gather personal information from anyone, including children.

Your control over your data

You are always in full control. To erase everything, use the Start over button inside the app, clear this site's stored data in your browser settings, or uninstall the app from your device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the new version will appear on this page with an updated date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy or the app? Email support@life168.org.
