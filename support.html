Common questions

Do I need to save my answers?

No. The app saves everything automatically in your browser as you go. When you return on the same device and browser, your answers are still there.

How do I clear everything and start fresh?

Use the Start over button near the top of the app. It erases every answer on your device (this cannot be undone), so it asks you to click twice to confirm.

Does it work without an internet connection?

Yes. Once you have opened the app on your device, it works offline. The video and podcast links need a connection.

How do I print my results or save them as a PDF?

Use your browser's print option (on a computer, press Ctrl and P, or use the browser menu). The page is set up to print cleanly, and your action plan fits on one page. Choose "Save as PDF" in the print window to keep a copy.

I turned on reminders but did not get a notification.

The most reliable reminder is the calendar file. In Part 6, turn on reminders and tap "Add reminders to my calendar." Your phone or computer calendar will then remind you on the 30, 60, and 90 day dates, even when the app is closed.

My answers disappeared.

Answers are stored per device and per browser. They will not appear if you switch to a different device or browser, use private or incognito mode, or clear your browser data. There is no account and nothing is stored online, so answers cannot be recovered once cleared.

Is my information private?

Yes. Everything stays on your own device and is never uploaded. See the Privacy Policy for details.
