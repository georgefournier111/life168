WHAT IS IN THIS FOLDER
  index.html            The complete survey app (one file, works offline once installed)
  manifest.webmanifest  App identity: name, colors, icons (what makes it installable)
  sw.js                 Service worker: caches the app so it works with no connection
  icons\                App icons for home screens and stores

HOW TO PUT IT ONLINE (pick one, both are free)

  Option A: Netlify Drop (fastest, about 2 minutes)
    1. Go to https://app.netlify.com/drop in your browser
    2. Drag this whole folder onto the page
    3. It gives you a live https address immediately
    4. You can set a nicer site name (or connect your own domain) in Site settings

  Option B: GitHub Pages
    1. Create a free account at github.com and make a new repository
    2. Upload the contents of this folder to the repository
    3. In the repository: Settings > Pages > deploy from the main branch
    4. Your site appears at https://YOURNAME.github.io/REPOSITORY

HOW PEOPLE INSTALL IT AS AN APP
  iPhone or iPad: open the address in Safari, tap the Share button,
    then "Add to Home Screen"
  Android: open the address in Chrome, tap the three dot menu,
    then "Add to Home screen" (or tap the Install prompt if it appears)
  It opens full screen with the 168 icon, works offline, and each
  person's answers stay saved on their own device.

TO UPDATE THE APP LATER
  Replace index.html with the new version and change the cache name in
  sw.js from "wlll168-v1" to "wlll168-v2" (then v3, and so on) so
  installed copies pick up the update.

NEXT STEP FOR GOOGLE PLAY (when ready)
  Once the site is live at a public https address, go to
  https://www.pwabuilder.com , enter your address, and it generates the
  Android package you upload to the Google Play Console ($25 one time).
